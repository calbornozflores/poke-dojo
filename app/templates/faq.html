Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Poke Dojo.

What is Poke Dojo?

Poke Dojo is your personal Pokémon training ground — a quiz app that covers all 1025 Pokémon. Test your knowledge across five Dojo training modes, battle a friend or your own Shadow in the Arena, and face the same mystery Pokémon as trainers worldwide in the Daily Challenge. Your progress is tracked, your strengths and weaknesses revealed, and your journey charted over time.

What game modes are available?

Dojo (5 modes):
· Name It — Easy: see the full artwork, type the Pokémon name
· Name It — Medium: same challenge, higher bar to earn full marks
· Name It — Hard: only the silhouette — identify it from the shadows
· Guess the Number: name and artwork shown, enter its place in the Pokédex
· Guess the Type — Easy: pick its primary type from a handful of choices
· Guess the Type — Hard: uncover every type it carries

Battle Arena:
· VS Mode: two trainers, one keyboard — a silhouette slowly reveals itself
· Solo Challenge: survive endless rounds against a tightening clock and your own Shadow
· Daily Challenge: one mystery Pokémon per day, shared with every trainer on the planet

What is the EVO score?

Your EVO score is a living measure of your overall trainer level — it rises as you improve and dips when you slack off. Play any mode, finish a session, and watch it shift. The Trainer Journey page charts its path over time so you can see how far you've come.

What is the Professor Oak Analysis?

After enough sessions in a mode, Professor Oak studies your training history and reveals your blind spots — the types, generations, or stages of evolution that still elude you. Once unlocked, the Dojo quietly steers more of those tricky Pokémon your way, so you sharpen exactly where it counts. Check your Profile page to see the verdict.

How does the Daily Challenge work?

Every day at midnight a new mystery Pokémon is chosen — the same one for every trainer worldwide. Guess any Pokémon name and you'll receive a distance score that tells you how close you are. The lower the score, the warmer you're getting.

🟩 Very close   🟨 Getting warmer   🟥 Far away

Your guesses are sorted closest-first. Find it before the day ends — tomorrow brings a new mystery.

How does sign-in with Google work?

Signing in with Google lets you claim a permanent trainer nickname that appears on the global leaderboard and Battle Arena rankings. You choose your own nickname — your Google email is never displayed or shared anywhere in the app.

Is my data private?

Your trainer nickname is the only thing other players ever see — it appears on the leaderboard and Arena rankings. No email address, real name, or account details are displayed or shared anywhere. Your game history lives on the server, quietly powering your profile and journey chart.

I found a bug — what do I do?

Please open an issue on GitHub: github.com/calbornozflores/poke-dojo/issues . Describe what you expected, what happened, and any steps to reproduce it. You can also reach out via the Contact page.
